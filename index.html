Admissions

B.E./B.TECH Lateral Entry Admissions

Lateral Entry Admissions into various programs are as per the norms laid down by the Department of Technical Education, Government of Tamil Nadu and Anna University. The program is designed for candidates with a 3-year diploma in engineering (10+3 pattern) or an equivalent degree to get direct admission to the 2nd year B.E./B.Tech program.
